Welcome to our Mock Recommendation System!

This recommendation system is designed for internal learning to understand the STELLA architecture. It utilizes the RELISH corpus and is developed with an aim to incorporate the STELLA framework and gain insights into its integration for future recommendation systems in production.

You can use the search bar to find articles by entering the PubMed IDs. For example, you can look for articles using PMIDs like 29053960, 29587830, or 21725296.
